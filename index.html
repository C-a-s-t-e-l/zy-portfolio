Featured Paper

Intelligence Dominance Grounded on Gardner's Multiple Intelligence Theory

A comprehensive study among Grade 11 Senior High School Students of Iloilo City National High School

Abastract:This study investigates the biases and hierarchical perceptions within the Philippine Senior High School curriculum, specifically in the context of academic and non-academic tracks. It examines how societal and institutional attitudes often elevate certain tracks, such as STEM, above others, leading to issues of discrimination and unequal treatment among students. Drawing from Gardner's theory of multiple intelligences, the study emphasizes the value of diverse student strengths and advocates for educational frameworks that honor and develop individual talents across all fields.
